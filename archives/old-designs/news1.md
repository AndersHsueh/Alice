**Headline:** Biden administration to end Trump-era policy that shielded oil and gas drillers from royalty fees
**Summary:** The Biden administration is planning to change a policy that protected oil and gas drillers from royalty fees, which could potentially increase government revenue but may also affect drillers' profits.
**Translation:** 拜登政府计划结束特朗普时期保护石油和天然气钻井商免受版税的政策。这一政策可能会增加政府的收入，但也可能影响石油和天然气钻井商的利润。
